# MVP scope

## Goal

Deliver a testable operations MVP for a single property: managers set context and work, staff see and complete assigned execution, and important signals are visible without building a reporting product.

## Administrator / manager (directional)

- Dispatch-style daily context (brief, watchlist).
- Task/card creation with assignment, due date/time, priority, and draft vs published behavior.
- Small template set for recurring operational work.
- Checklists on cards, with optional enforcement before completion.
- Activity-style feed of recent operational changes.
- Staff list, roles, notes, and outcomes over time.
- Card detail as the place work happens (status, assignee, checklist, complete / blocked / help).
- Stored engagement signals to support later “admin cards” and accountability.

## Operator / staff (directional)

- Simple sign-in to reach assigned work.
- Daily brief and “since last shift” style awareness.
- Interact on assigned cards (complete, comment, attach, block, request help).
- Lightweight upward notes to management when needed.

## Explicitly later

Chat, deep analytics dashboards, complex scheduling, recruiting, payroll/timeclock, geo tracking, deep PMS sync, and AI-generated cards.
